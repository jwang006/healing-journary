HEALING JOURNEY

In the huge digital information age, we always accept it and are always overloaded. How can we solve our health problems? The best choice is to go to a “doctor” for treatment. Can we act on our own to improve our health problems without the help of drugs and doctors? I want this space to be our doctor and guide us to examine and treat ourselves.

The best way to fix negative moods is face it and take the positive action. Just as normal as going to the doctor when we get sick. I want the space in this project to be our doctor to diagnose and heal our emotional sickness. Walk into this space and then actively participate in the tests and treatments so that the space will guide us to re-understand ourselves conversely to get ultimate triumph, which is making peace by ourselves and getting tranquility eventually.

Sound is always around us, big or small. Often we only respond to the voices we care about, such as conversations and musics? What about the voices we ignore? They may be waiting for our response.Like singer, Henry, he loves every single sound. In your busy life, have you stopped to listen to the voices around us? Do you hear the voice of urban renew and development? Are childhood songs remembered in your ears? I want to use this work to translate what happened in the community I once lived in with sound, and respond to my memory for this place.
